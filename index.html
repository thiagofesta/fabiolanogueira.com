fabíola nogueira

Born in the city of Toledo, State of Paraná Brazil, was a place where Fabiola Nogueira began his career as Make Up Artist.

Since 2013 she works professionally as a Makeup Artist, and in that time, she had opportunities to work with editorials and photo book with a reputed photographer, Rodrigo Vipych.

Besides that she also owned a makeup studio in Toledo to serve her customers for weddings, graduation and other social events.

Currently she lives in San Francisco, and works as freelancer.
